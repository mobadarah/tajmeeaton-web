# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developers, builders, and open-source enthusiasts looking for Arabic-language technology projects. Two primary modes: targeted search (finding a specific tool for a concrete need — e.g., "an Arabic spell checker" or "Quranic text analysis library") and serendipitous browsing (discovering what exists in the Arabic OSS ecosystem). The user is typically at a computer, task-oriented, and values speed and clarity over visual flourish.

## Product Purpose

A curated, searchable directory of Arabic open-source technology projects. It exists to solve the discovery problem in the Arabic OSS space: projects are scattered across GitHub, niche forums, and personal pages, and there is no single authoritative reference. Success means being the first place the Arabic developer community goes to find, evaluate, and contribute to Arabic-language open-source tools.

## Brand Personality

Clean, Authoritative, Modern. The voice is knowledgeable but not academic — precise, helpful, and confident. This is a reference, not a blog. Every word earns its place.

## Anti-references

Not over-designed, not cluttered, not flashy. Avoid the SaaS-cream monoculture, noisy backgrounds, decorative illustrations, and anything that competes with the project data itself. Clean and functional — the content is the hero.

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over decoration.** The projects are the product. The interface steps back and lets the data speak — generous whitespace, clear hierarchy, no ornamental flourishes.
2. **Scanable at a glance.** Every user comes with a goal (search or discover). The interface must support rapid scanning through filter controls, prominent search, and information-dense list/card layouts that surface the most important fields first.
3. **Bilingual by design.** Arabic-first RTL with English data alongside. The layout must feel native in Arabic, handle mixed-language content gracefully, and never compromise readability for either script.
4. **Trustworthy through restraint.** Authority is earned through consistency, precision, and restraint — not through ornament. Clean typography, predictable patterns, and careful alignment signal curation quality.
5. **Fast by default.** A directory tool must load fast and feel instant. Performance is a design feature: minimal JS, optimized assets, instant search feedback.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Sufficient color contrast (4.5:1 body, 3:1 large text), keyboard-navigable filters and search, screen-reader-friendly link text, and reduced-motion support for any animations.
